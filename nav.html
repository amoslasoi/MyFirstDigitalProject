About us

The study of periodontal disease among expectant mothers is important because the disease can cause a lot of anxiety and psychological trauma to them. In addition, these future mothers tend to think that any of the dreaded painful measures to treat their gingival or dental condition may precipitate abortion (Kaimenyi, 1985). Therefore, they need to be educated on the safety of the treatment. Hence if the expectant mothers who are also future mothers are educated about their dental condition or gingival condition, they will be in a good position to positively influence their family about oral health.
